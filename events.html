Events organized by Indians in Dresden

Dresden has a very small Indian community, mainly centered around the Technical University of Dresden. There are hardly any one living here permanently. The community almost entirely comprises of students and post-docs. A few people however, work in companies like Infeneon and AMD. I am listing some of the events organized by Indians as an archive.
