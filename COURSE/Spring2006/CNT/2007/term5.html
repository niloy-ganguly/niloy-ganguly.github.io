Studies on Orthographic Networks

This is a continuation of one of the works done last time (i.e., you get some pieces of codes for free!).

Task 1: Construct the pronunciation dictionary using G2P for several languages including French, English, Hindi, Bangla, German.

Task 2: Construct the phonemic neighbors network for each language

Task 3: Measure important topological properties of these n/ws and show that they are almost similar.

Task 4: Construct the orthographic n/ws for French/German (we already have the same for English/Bengali and Hindi)

Task 4: Show that the orthographic n/ws are similar for Hindi/Bengali and Eng/French/German

Task 5: Try to relate these differences with orthographic systems.

    • Data Source – Google (French/German corpus + G2P/pronunciation dict.)
    • References – You are supposed to vide references from the works of Ferrer i Cancho and Ricard Solé and the works by Vitevich. (More references from contact persons)
